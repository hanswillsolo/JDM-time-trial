JDM Time Trial

JDM Time Trial is a Japanese domestic motor themed time trail racing game by Is it Worth it Games taking you on a tour through the night life and JDM scene of Japan. Race through the back streets and mountains of Japan; racing against the clock. Beat the clock in classic JDM motors such as the Nissan Skyline R34 and the Mark 4 Toyota Supra.

Info

Is it Worth it Games is a game development studio based in Newcastle-under-Lyme. We decieded to start this company as we want to bring people a high quality at low cost this is something that we thrive to achieve.

Future Conten

The future content of JDM Time Trial will consist of a mixture of free and paid. There will be content such as more cars and maps.
