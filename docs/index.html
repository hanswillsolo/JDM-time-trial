JDM Time Trial

JDM Time Trial is a Japanese domestic motor themed time trail racing game taking you on a tour through the night life and JDM scene of Japan. Race through the back streets and mountains of Japan; racing against the clock. Beat the clock in classic JDM motors such as the Nissan Skyline R34 and the Mark 4 Toyota Supra

Info

Greetings fellow JDM enthusiasts this game is for you. Have you ever felt like there isn't any JDM style games out there to show off your drifting or your drag racing well this game is for you. You can drift in it race via the in game timer.
